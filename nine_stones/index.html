📜 How to Play Navakankari

🎯 Objective

Reduce your opponent to 2 pieces or block all their moves.

🎲 Game Phases

Phase 1: Placing

Players take turns placing one piece at a time on any empty intersection. Each player has 9 pieces.

Phase 2: Moving

After all pieces are placed, move one piece to an adjacent empty intersection along a line.

Phase 3: Flying

When you have only 3 pieces left, you can "fly" - move to any empty intersection!

⭐ Forming a Mill

A mill is three pieces of the same color in a straight line. Form a mill to remove one opponent's piece (except pieces in a mill, unless no other pieces are available).

🏆 Winning

  • Reduce your opponent to 2 pieces
  • Block all opponent's pieces so they cannot move
